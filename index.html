01General Rules

General Rules

The vast majority of the rules are setup in Sleeper and are visible there, the following rules are for things not explicitly documented in the league settings.

  1. Payouts are for first through third finishers.
  2. All winners will have the following season's buy-in witheld from their winnings.
  3. Mike is never allowed to win a championship, preferably not even allowed in the post-season.

02Draft Order

Draft Order

  1. Draft order is set to inverse of the final standings from the year before, except for the first draft position.
  2. First draft position is decided by the winner of the toilet bowl, which is technically the #9 ranked team in final standings.
  3. The rookie draft is linear, meaning first draft position would have draft picks 1.1, 2.1, 3.1, etc.
